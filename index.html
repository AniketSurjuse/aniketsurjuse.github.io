About Me

Developer with hands-on experience building AI tools focused on performance, clarity, and practical impact.

I got into AI because I was genuinely curious about how it works and what it can do. The idea that intelligent systems can solve real-world problems really stuck with me, and that’s what I’ve been working toward since. Right now, I’m building with large language models and automating ML workflows to make things a little more useful and efficient.

I like to understand things by building them from scratch. It’s not always the fastest way, but it helps me learn deeply. Whether it’s a model, a system, or a tool, I try to break it down and rebuild it so I know how it really works.

Outside of work, I enjoy movies, sketching, reading, and exploring new places. These keep me grounded and curious in different ways. I’m hoping to keep learning, keep building, and be part of something that has a real impact.
